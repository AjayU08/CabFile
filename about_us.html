Welcome To New Carmel High School

New Carmel High School has been established in the year 2004, as Co Educational Institution for Nursery, Primary and High School under the Management of Chinmayee Educational Trust. Initially the school was started from Nursery level and elevated up to High School. The aim of our institute is to give good quality education to the children of the surrounding areas. Our mission is to educate children through a comprehensive academic curriculum supported by critical thinking as well as better communication.

OUR OBJECTIVES

Fostering in students a sense of pride in our culture and heritage. Exposure to knowledge among students through Curricular and extra Curricular activities. To develop the intrinsic qualities of the students. To mould students into good citizens through morals, education discipline and encouragement

School Responsibilities

  • 1. Quality Education with discipline
  • 2. Concern about each child
  • 3. Individual personalized attention and focusing on each and every child
  • 4. We will conduct career counseling every year for out going grade 10th std students
  • 5. Emphasis on life skills that go beyond their curriculum

Accreditations & Affiliations

New Carmel High School is having accreditation and affiliation with Karnataka Secondary Education Examination Board to run classes from Nursery to 10th State Board.
